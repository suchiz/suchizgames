Introduction

I am Julien Hongsavanh. I am 26 years old. I am graduated from a MSc Computer Graphics and Image Analysis. I worked one year for Safran Engineering Services. I was in charge of recognition, detection and segmentation of surface defects using convolutional neural networks (MaskRCNN and Yolact++). Both were remote controlled with an android application using a server/client model. Yolact++ was working in livestream.

I have been passionate about computers since I was a child. It started with video games, then at the age of 13, I programmed my first game in C. At that age, I was already convinced that I would work in computer science later on. Then, it's only in my second year of bachelor's degree that I am fascinated by digital imaging. Indeed, digital imaging is the field in which I would like to work because it is omnipresent in everyday life and its needs are increasing while being different from day to day. It is this fascinating and challenging diversity that makes this field the most attractive to me. For instance for image processing, there is Big Data and IoT which require the refinement of images perceived by capture devices such as surveillance cameras or satellites. And for computer graphics, there is more and more realistic games, or realistic animations. Consequently, I chose options such as "Introduction to Computer Graphics and Image Processing" as early as my degree in order to specialise in the field. To pursue this goal, I decided to do my first internship in Montreal in digital imaging to confirm my professional project. In this internship oriented towards computer graphics, I first had to implement a translation function to an existing tool on an open source image editing software in C++ language. I also made an exchange year at Pazmany Peter Catholic University in Budapest, an university member of the European Image processing and Computer Vision Master degree. And finally, I finished with my Master of Science in Computer Graphics and Image Analysis. Today, my passion for computers and my taste for innovation and challenge is reflected in numerous participations in Hackathons on various subjects, such as video game development, software development or artificial intelligence. This also translates into the development of a video game for Android in my spare time. Check out the Suchiz Games tab for more informations on these !
