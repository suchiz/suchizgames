Get to know me a little bit more

Curious - Open minded - Challenger - Dedicated - Creative

I am passionate about technologies since I am little and always loved challenges. As long as I remember, I always have been very competitive since I started sports at the age of 6. These two characteristics are expressed today through many participations in hackathons. And yes, althought I am a very autonomous person (as testifies my decision to go freelance), I am easily going with people and know how to work in a group (still confirmed in hackathons).
More over, I love to create things. It began with my parents who play in a band since I am little and I grew up with this vibe. It gave me the chance to try, to compose, and to record my own songs at home ! My curiosity also had me create a lot of banners on photoshop. Crafting, is one of my best feeling, I think that is why I enjoy so much programming. Also, I developed some games during my free time. This is how Suchiz Games was borned :).
